## Paid Time Off (PTO)

Volta Coffee Co. provides a unified PTO bank. There is no separate distinction between sick days and vacation days.

### Accrual Rates

| Employment Tier | Annual PTO | Accrual Rate |
|----------------|-----------|-------------|
| Barista / Retail Staff | 15 days | 1.25 days/month |
| Shift Lead / Assistant Manager | 18 days | 1.50 days/month |
| Store Manager | 20 days | 1.67 days/month |
| Corporate Staff (non-exempt) | 20 days | 1.67 days/month |
| Corporate Staff (exempt) | Unlimited | N/A |

### PTO Policies

- PTO begins accruing on the first day of employment but cannot be used until after 90 days.
- Maximum carryover is 5 days per calendar year. Unused PTO beyond 5 days expires on December 31.
- PTO requests for retail staff must be submitted at least 14 days in advance via Homebase.
- Requests during blackout periods (Thanksgiving week, December 20–31) require Store Manager approval and are granted on a seniority basis.
- Corporate staff submit PTO through Gusto.

## Remote Work Policy

- **Retail and production staff:** Not eligible for remote work.
- **Corporate staff:** Hybrid schedule — minimum 3 days in-office (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday). Monday and Friday are remote-optional.
- **Fully remote exceptions:** Granted only for roles explicitly designated as remote in the job posting, or by VP-level approval for medical accommodation.
- All remote work must occur within the United States. International remote work requires written approval from the CFO due to tax implications.

## Expense Reimbursement

### Eligible Expenses

- Business travel (flights, hotels, ground transportation)
- Client meals and team meals (manager approval required for amounts over $75)
- Professional development (conferences, courses — pre-approval required from direct manager)
- Home office equipment for corporate staff (up to $500 one-time, then $200/year)

### Process

1. Submit receipts within 30 days of the expense via Ramp.
2. Attach itemized receipts — credit card statements alone are not accepted.
3. Manager approval is required for all expenses over $100.
4. Reimbursement is processed on the next payroll cycle after approval.

### Per Diem Rates (Domestic Travel)

| Category | Rate |
|---------|------|
| Meals | $65/day |
| Lodging | Actual cost, up to $250/night (NYC/SF: $350/night) |
| Incidentals | $15/day |

## Anti-Harassment Policy

Volta Coffee Co. maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, discrimination, and retaliation. This covers all protected classes under federal and applicable state/local law, including race, color, religion, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, and veteran status.

All complaints should be directed to the People Operations team at people@voltacoffee.com or reported anonymously through the EthicsPoint hotline (1-800-555-0147). Investigations will be initiated within 48 hours of a report.

## Benefits Overview

- **Health Insurance:** Medical, dental, and vision through Aetna. Eligible after 60 days for employees working 30+ hours/week. Volta covers 80% of employee premiums and 50% of dependent premiums.
- **401(k):** Available after 6 months. Volta matches 50% of contributions up to 4% of salary.
- **Coffee Benefit:** All employees receive a free 12oz bag of whole bean coffee per week and 50% discount on all retail purchases.
- **Parental Leave:** 12 weeks paid for birth parents, 6 weeks paid for non-birth parents, after 1 year of employment.
