# Volta Coffee Co. — Brewing Standards & Formulas

**Document ID:** BREW-001  
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-15  
**Owner:** Quality Assurance Team  
**Classification:** Internal Use Only

## Espresso Extraction Parameters

All espresso-based beverages at Volta Coffee Co. must conform to the following extraction standards. Deviations require written approval from the Head of Quality.

### Standard Espresso

- **Dose:** 18.5g ± 0.3g
- **Yield:** 38g ± 2g
- **Ratio:** 1:2.05
- **Extraction Time:** 26–30 seconds
- **Water Temperature:** 200°F ± 2°F (93.3°C)
- **Pressure:** 9 bar at the group head

### Ristretto

- **Dose:** 18.5g ± 0.3g
- **Yield:** 28g ± 2g
- **Ratio:** 1:1.5
- **Extraction Time:** 22–26 seconds

### Lungo

- **Dose:** 18.5g ± 0.3g
- **Yield:** 55g ± 3g
- **Ratio:** 1:3.0
- **Extraction Time:** 35–45 seconds

## Batch Brew (Drip) Parameters

Batch brew is prepared using Curtis G4 ThermoPro brewers. All locations must calibrate weekly using the protocol in document QA-012.

- **Coffee-to-Water Ratio:** 1:16.5 by weight
- **Grind Setting:** Baratza Forte BG at setting 7M (medium-coarse)
- **Water Temperature:** 202°F ± 1°F (94.4°C)
- **Brew Time:** 4:30–5:30 for a full batch (1.9L)
- **TDS Target:** 1.25–1.40%
- **Extraction Yield Target:** 19.5–21.5%

## Cold Brew Concentrate

Cold brew is prepared in Toddy commercial systems at the central production facility and distributed to locations weekly.

- **Coffee-to-Water Ratio:** 1:5 by weight
- **Grind Setting:** Coarse (Ditting 807 at setting 8.5)
- **Steep Time:** 18–22 hours at 38–42°F (3.3–5.6°C)
- **Filtration:** Double paper filter, then 50-micron inline filter
- **Dilution at Service:** 1:1 concentrate-to-water (still) or 1:0.8 (nitro)
- **Shelf Life:** 7 days refrigerated from production date

## Water Quality Requirements

All Volta locations must use reverse osmosis water remineralized to the following spec:

- **Total Dissolved Solids (TDS):** 120–150 ppm
- **Calcium Hardness:** 50–70 ppm as CaCO3
- **Total Alkalinity:** 40–50 ppm as CaCO3
- **pH:** 7.0 ± 0.3
- **Chlorine:** 0 ppm (carbon block filtration required)

Water testing is performed monthly by the facilities team using LaMotte ColorQ Pro kits. Results are logged in the Water Quality Tracker (Notion database, link in QA-012).

## Milk Steaming Standards

- **Starting Temperature:** Milk must be below 40°F (4.4°C) from refrigeration
- **Target Temperature:** 140–150°F (60–65.6°C) for service
- **Maximum Temperature:** 160°F (71.1°C) — milk exceeding this must be discarded
- **Texture:** Microfoam with bubbles no larger than 1mm; glossy, paint-like consistency
- **Alternative Milks:** Oat milk (Oatly Barista) steams to 145°F max; almond steams to 150°F max

## Grinder Calibration

Espresso grinders (Mazzer Major V Electronic) must be calibrated:
- At opening each day
- After any hopper refill with a different lot
- If extraction time drifts outside the 26–30 second window
- After any maintenance or burr change

Calibration procedure: Pull three consecutive shots within spec, discarding the first after any grind adjustment. Log final setting in the Shift Calibration Sheet.
