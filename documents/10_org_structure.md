# Volta Coffee Co. — Organizational Structure

**Document ID:** ORG-001  
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-01  
**Owner:** People Operations  
**Classification:** Internal Use Only

## Company Overview

Volta Coffee Co. is a specialty coffee company based in Brooklyn, New York. Founded in 2017, Volta operates a roastery, four retail locations (with a fifth opening Q2 2026), an e-commerce DTC business, and a small wholesale program.

**Total Headcount:** 68 employees (as of March 2026)

## Department Structure

### Executive (3 people)
- CEO, CTO, Executive Assistant

### Engineering (6 people)
Reports to: James Okonkwo (CTO)
- 1 Engineering Manager
- 3 Software Engineers (full-stack)
- 1 Data Engineer
- 1 IT Support Specialist (split between engineering and retail ops)

The Engineering team maintains all internal systems (see ENG-001 for details). The team operates on 2-week sprint cycles with planning on Mondays and retros on alternate Fridays.

### Retail Operations (42 people)
Reports to: Sarah Kim (VP of Retail Operations)

**Per-Location Staffing:**
| Role | Williamsburg | Park Slope | LES | Red Hook Café |
|------|-------------|------------|-----|---------------|
| Store Manager | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| Assistant Manager | 1 | 1 | 0 | 0 |
| Shift Lead | 3 | 2 | 2 | 1 |
| Barista | 8 | 6 | 5 | 3 |

The Midtown location (Q2 2026 opening) will add approximately 12 staff: 1 Store Manager, 1 Assistant Manager, 3 Shift Leads, and 7 Baristas.

### Coffee (Sourcing & Quality) (5 people)
Reports to: Diego Ramirez (VP of Coffee)
- 1 Head Roaster (Ben Alvarez)
- 2 Production Roasters
- 1 Green Coffee Buyer / QA Analyst
- 1 Roastery Operations Assistant

### People Operations (2 people)
Reports to: Priya Patel
- 1 HR Coordinator
- Priya handles strategic people ops, recruiting, and benefits administration

### Finance (2 people)
Reports to: Tom Nguyen
- 1 Staff Accountant
- Tom handles financial planning, investor relations, and vendor negotiations

### Marketing & E-Commerce (4 people)
Reports to: Rachel Morrison
- 1 Brand Designer
- 1 Social Media & Content Manager
- 1 E-Commerce Operations Manager (manages Shopify, Recharge, ShipStation)
- 1 Wholesale Account Manager

### Facilities (2 people)
Reports to: Sarah Kim (dotted line to James Okonkwo for technical issues)
- 1 Facilities Manager (responsible for all locations and the roastery)
- 1 Facilities Technician

## Decision-Making Authority

| Decision Type | Authority Level | Approval Required From |
|--------------|----------------|----------------------|
| Daily operations (scheduling, ordering) | Store Manager | None |
| Hiring (retail) | Store Manager | VP Retail + People Ops |
| Hiring (corporate) | Department Head | CEO + People Ops |
| Equipment purchases under $1,000 | Store Manager | VP Retail |
| Equipment purchases $1,000–$10,000 | VP or Department Head | CEO |
| Equipment purchases over $10,000 | CEO | Board notification |
| New menu items | VP of Coffee | CEO approval |
| Pricing changes | CEO | Head of Finance review |
| Vendor contracts over $25,000/year | CEO | Head of Finance review |
| New store openings | CEO | Board approval |
