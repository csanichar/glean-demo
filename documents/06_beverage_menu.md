## Core Espresso Beverages

All espresso beverages use the standard extraction parameters defined in BREW-001. Default milk is whole milk unless the customer specifies an alternative.

### Americano
- Double espresso (38g yield)
- 8oz hot water (for 12oz cup) or 10oz (for 16oz cup)
- Espresso pulled directly into cup, water added after
- Iced: Espresso over ice, cold water to fill

### Latte
| Size | Espresso Shots | Milk Volume | Foam Depth |
|------|---------------|-------------|------------|
| 8oz | 1 | 6oz | ¼ inch |
| 12oz | 2 | 9oz | ¼ inch |
| 16oz | 2 | 13oz | ¼ inch |

### Cappuccino
| Size | Espresso Shots | Milk Volume | Foam Depth |
|------|---------------|-------------|------------|
| 8oz | 1 | 4oz | 1 inch |
| 12oz | 2 | 7oz | 1 inch |

Cappuccinos are only available in 8oz and 12oz. Do not serve a 16oz cappuccino — the milk-to-foam ratio cannot be maintained.

### Flat White
- 12oz only
- Double ristretto (28g yield, per BREW-001)
- Whole milk steamed to 140°F with minimal foam (⅛ inch)
- Latte art required

### Cortado
- 4oz only
- Double espresso (38g yield)
- Equal parts espresso and steamed milk (approximately 2oz milk)
- Served in a 4.5oz Gibraltar glass
- No foam

## Signature Beverages

### The Volta (House Signature)
- Double espresso
- 1oz house-made vanilla bean syrup (recipe in SYRUP-001)
- Oat milk (Oatly Barista), steamed to 140°F
- Topped with a light dusting of cinnamon
- Available hot or iced
- 12oz and 16oz

### Honey Cardamom Latte
- Double espresso
- 0.75oz honey cardamom syrup (recipe in SYRUP-001)
- Whole milk steamed to 145°F
- Pinch of ground cardamom on top
- Available hot or iced
- 12oz and 16oz
- **Seasonal:** Available September through March

### Cold Brew Tonic
- 4oz cold brew concentrate (diluted per BREW-001 cold brew specs)
- 8oz tonic water (Fever-Tree Indian Tonic)
- Served over ice in a 16oz cup
- Orange peel garnish
- **Seasonal:** Available April through September

### Matcha Latte (Non-Coffee)
- 2 scoops (4g) ceremonial-grade matcha (Ippodo brand, stored in refrigerator)
- 2oz hot water (175°F) — whisk until no clumps remain
- Steamed milk of choice to fill
- Available hot or iced
- 12oz and 16oz
- **Note:** This is not a coffee beverage. If a customer asks for a "matcha espresso," clarify that we do not combine matcha and espresso due to conflicting flavor profiles. Offer them separately.

## Batch Brew Service

- Batch brew is offered in 12oz and 16oz cups only.
- Two origins available at all times: one washed, one natural process.
- Rotate batch brew offerings weekly per the Roasting Schedule (published every Monday in #retail-ops Slack channel).
- Batch brew is held for a maximum of 90 minutes after brewing. Set a timer on the airpot. After 90 minutes, discard and brew fresh.
- If a customer requests decaf drip, offer a decaf pour-over as an alternative (V60, using the pour-over recipe below).

## Pour-Over (V60)

- **Dose:** 22g
- **Water:** 360g at 205°F
- **Ratio:** 1:16.4
- **Grind:** Medium-fine (EK43 setting 7.5)
- **Brew Time:** 3:00–3:30 total

### Method
1. Rinse filter with hot water, discard rinse water.
2. Add grounds, create a flat bed.
3. Bloom: Pour 60g water in a spiral, wait 30 seconds.
4. First pour: Add water to 200g by 1:15.
5. Second pour: Add water to 360g by 2:00.
6. Drawdown should complete by 3:00–3:30.
7. Swirl the finished cup gently before serving.

Pour-overs are charged at a $2.00 premium above the batch brew price. Notify the customer of the 4-minute preparation time when they order.
